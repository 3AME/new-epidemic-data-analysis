Project Description

The new coronavirus pneumonia (COVID-19, referred to as "new coronary pneumonia") epidemic raged in many countries around the world, on March 11, 2020, the World Health Organization (WHO) officially announced that the new coronary pneumonia as a global pandemic.

In the global fight against the new coronavirus epidemic, unprecedented large-scale epidemic data has been produced.

The use of big data-level artificial intelligence analysis techniques and methods can meet the needs of monitoring the virus infection, monitoring the development of the epidemic, and assisting in the deployment of rescue materials, etc. Good assistance in epidemic prevention and control.

Function realization


As a government and enterprise user, I hope that the interface has a visual world map, so that the analysis of the spatial and temporal distribution pattern of epidemic situation and the evaluation of epidemic prevention and control measures are concise.

As a government and enterprise user, I hope the interface has a visual map of some countries (China, the United States, Italy, etc.), so that the situation of the epidemic can be monitored directly.

As a civilian, I hope that the interface has data on the epidemic situation in the political regions of various countries, so that it can compare the spread of differences among different regions.

As a government and enterprise user, I hope to detect abnormal propagation events through intuitive charts, so that I can reasonably formulate communication management and control strategies.

As a public health management user, I hope that the system has a certain forecasting ability, so that it can predict the development trend and key nodes of the epidemic.

As a public media, I hope to get a preliminary understanding of the development of the epidemic situation in a certain place through the sentiment orientation of social media.

As a user in the scientific research field, I hope to systematically understand the hot topics of new coronary pneumonia research, so that it is convenient for me to carry out my research work.

As a user in the field of medical research, I hope that the interface can be associated with multi-source data (population mobility, age structure, urban-rural structure, etc.), so that the epidemic situation propagation mode can be analyzed.

As an investor, I hope to fully correlate multi-source data (economic indicators, material procurement, online education, etc.) so that I can assess the impact of the epidemic on the national economy, enterprise production, education and training, etc.
